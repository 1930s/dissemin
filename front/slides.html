(Green) Open Access

  • Researchers can deposit their publications in open repositories, even when publishers continue to charge universities for subscriptions!
  • But this right is often not used by authors.
  • Using personal home pages is not enough.

↪ We want to help researchers deposit as many papers as possible.

We pinpoint your paywalled papers

and help you to liberate them

  • We build your publications list based on various metadata sources. Millions of papers are covered, in many disciplines
  • We try to find freely available copies.
  • We summarize the publishing policies.

We disambiguate them

  • Author names are ambiguous: we do our best to show only relevant papers

  • Papers are clustered by similarity and attributed to researchers based on various features
