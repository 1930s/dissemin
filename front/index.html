Wait, what?

Millions of papers are still locked behind paywalls. For many of them, their authors could have made them available in a repository, given the self-archiving policy of their publisher.

Dissemin spots these papers and helps their authors upload them quickly to mature and visible repositories such as HAL or Zenodo.

Can I use it?

Our platform can already be used by independent researchers. We are working with institutional partners to set up dedicated instances of the software for them. If you are interested in the project, please get in touch: hello@dissem.in.
